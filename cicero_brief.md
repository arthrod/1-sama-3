## Design Constraints (Inferred)
- **Palette:**

- **Product:** "Cicero" - Email-based legal document assistant. No dashboard. Converts messy email -> structured DOCX/PDF.
- **Motif:** Before/After (messy email -> clean doc).

## Design Rationale
- **Gap:** Since no visual representation exists, I am designing the **Landing Page** from scratch.
- **Focus:** Highlight the "No Dashboard" value proposition and the "Messy Email -> Clean Doc" transformation.
- **Pricing:** Introducing a tiered pricing model (Free/Pro/Firm) to give the product a "real" feel.
