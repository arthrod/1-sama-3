Settlement Agreement and Mutual Release

Case No. 24-CV-01928 â€¢ Last Edited 14 mins ago
1. Recitals

This Settlement Agreement and Mutual Release ("Agreement") is entered into as of October 14, 2024, by and between Smith Holdings, LLC ("Plaintiff") and Jones Industries, Inc. ("Defendant").

WHEREAS, Plaintiff filed a civil action against Defendant in the Superior Court of California, County of San Francisco, styled Smith Holdings, LLC v. Jones Industries, Inc., Case No. CGC-23-123456 (the "Action");

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to resolve the Action and all claims and disputes between them without further litigation, admission of liability, or expense;

2. Payment Terms

Defendant agrees to pay Plaintiff the sum of Two Million Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($2,500,000.00) (the "Settlement Amount") within thirty (30) days of the Effective Date. Payment shall be made by wire transfer to the trust account of Plaintiff's counsel.

Failure to make payment within the specified timeframe shall result in the accrual of interest at a rate of 10% per annum. The Parties agree that time is of the essence with respect to this payment obligation.

3. Release of Claims

Upon receipt of the Settlement Amount, Plaintiff hereby releases, acquits, and forever discharges Defendant and its officers, directors, employees, and agents from any and all claims, demands, damages, actions, causes of action, or suits of any kind or nature whatsoever, known or unknown, which Plaintiff now has or may hereafter have against Defendant, arising out of or relating to the facts alleged in the Action.

Plaintiff expressly waives the provisions of California Civil Code Section 1542, which provides: "A GENERAL RELEASE DOES NOT EXTEND TO CLAIMS THAT THE CREDITOR OR RELEASING PARTY DOES NOT KNOW OR SUSPECT TO EXIST IN HIS OR HER FAVOR AT THE TIME OF EXECUTING THE RELEASE AND THAT, IF KNOWN BY HIM OR HER, WOULD HAVE MATERIALLY AFFECTED HIS OR HER SETTLEMENT WITH THE DEBTOR OR RELEASED PARTY."

4. Confidentiality

The Parties agree to keep the terms and conditions of this Agreement confidential. Neither Party shall disclose the Settlement Amount to any third party, except as required by law or to their respective legal and financial advisors.
